Meditation Exercises

Try these meditation exercises to reduce stress and improve mental wellbeing:

1. Breathing Meditation (5 minutes)

Focus on your breath to calm your mind and body:

  • Sit comfortably with your back straight
  • Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths
  • Focus on the sensation of air moving in and out of your body
  • When your mind wanders, gently return your focus to your breath

2. Body Scan Meditation (10 minutes)

Bring awareness to each part of your body:

  • Lie down or sit comfortably
  • Close your eyes and bring attention to your toes
  • Slowly move your attention up through your body
  • Notice any sensations without judgment

3. Loving-Kindness Meditation (8 minutes)

Cultivate feelings of compassion and kindness:

  • Sit comfortably and close your eyes
  • Visualize yourself and repeat: "May I be happy, may I be healthy"
  • Extend these wishes to others in your life
  • Finally, extend them to all beings everywhere

For more guided meditations, visit UCLA Mindful or NHS Meditation Guide.
